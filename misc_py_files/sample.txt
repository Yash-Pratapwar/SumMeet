In the age of changing times, having and performing a team evaluation or communication which presents meaningful outcomes is of grieving importance.
Saving your time, and getting astounding results was one of the main aim of organizing any kind of meetings.
However to manually summarize the overall meeting in a short period of time is not feasible and efficient.
There is always a role of Artificial Intelligence in reducing human intervention and proposing a solution which makes a difficult task doable in an easy manner.
SumMeet is one of the solutions that can influence a meeting and its attendees by generating useful minutes of a held meeting and generate efficient and important points out of it.
Its helpful representation also makes it easier to understand, which can be shared with other systems, so as to increase efficiency of a working management.
