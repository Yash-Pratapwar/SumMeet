F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How SumMeet is unique?

    Every other tool in the market converts your audio into text, but no transcription tool gives you the overall summary of conversation or key aways. For an half hour call what do you need? 5000 words lengthy document or just 5-6 key takeaways that you can read/listen within couple of minutes. SumMeet is the only AI tool in the market that gives you key takeaways with great accuracy.

  • Does SumMeet support multiple languages?

    Currently, our tool supports English and Hindi to some extent. We are currently working to add other local and international languages.

  • How does SumMeet summarize your meetings?

    SumMeet uses Transformers based abstractive summarization model for summarizing the meetings. This model uses the concept of self- attention mechanism wherein the model relates different positions of a single sequence to compute a representation of the same sequence. The model then dynamically weighs the importance of each part of the input sequence, rather than using a fixed-length representation, and helps to capture long range dependencies. This mechanism helps to acheive state-of-the-art results in summarization.

  • How does SumMeet create the transcript of the meetings?

    SumMeet uses Whisper, which is an Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) system developed by openAI, for creating the transcripts of the meetings. The Whisper uses an encoder-decoder Transformer. The input audio is first split into 30-second chunks, converted into a log-Mel spectrogram and then passed into an encoder. A decoder is trained to predict the corresponding text caption, intermixed with special tokens that direct the single model to perform multilingual speech transcription.
